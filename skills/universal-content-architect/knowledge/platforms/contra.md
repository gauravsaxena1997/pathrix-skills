# Contra

Freelance platform that became a social network. Audience: independent designers, devs, AI builders, plus clients hiring them. Tone is closer to LinkedIn-pro than X-edgy, but more visual and less corporate. Posts double as portfolio extension and lead-gen surface.

## Platform DNA

- Portfolio-first network, community feed bolted on (relaunched late 2025 as full creative network)
- Zero commission means creators stay loyal; smaller, less saturated than IG/X
- Feed sits adjacent to your profile: every post is one click from your services and work
- Discovery happens via skill tags + Creator badge amplification, not pure follower graph
- Contra may republish top posts to their own social channels (free distribution upside)

## What Contra rewards (signals)

1. **Consistency over volume** : 2+ posts/week is the floor for Creator badge consideration
2. **Originality** : Contra explicitly screens for non-recycled content for the badge
3. **Visual quality** : the feed is design-forward; low-quality screenshots starve
4. **Skill-tagged posts** : tags are the primary discovery vector, not hashtags
5. **Pro-member referrals from your post** : conversions earn the platform money, so it surfaces high-converting posts more

## What Contra starves

- Pure text rants (no visual = no reach on a visual feed)
- Recycled IG memes without context
- Engagement bait ("comment YES if you agree") : feels off-brand for the audience
- Self-promo without a build/lesson attached (Upwork-style "available for hire" posts flop)
- Cross-posted IG vertical 9:16 without resizing : Contra feed is mostly desktop, square or 4:5 reads better
- Posting only when launching : kills Creator badge eligibility

## Post types that work (ranked)

| Format | Why it works | Use when |
|---|---|---|
| **Build log carousel** (3-8 slides: problem - process - result + screenshots) | Reads like a portfolio item; clients screenshot it | Shipped a feature, finished a project, refactor with before/after |
| **Short demo video** (15-60s, screen recording + voiceover or text overlay) | Watch time signal + proof of skill | Working SaaS, animation, AI agent demo |
| **Single hero image + 2-3 paragraph caption** (case study lite) | Lowest effort, still visual | Quick win, lesson, mini-launch |
| **Tutorial post** (numbered steps, code block, screenshots) | Clients judge expertise; peers save it | Niche technique you've earned |
| **Behind-the-build journal** (desk photo, Loom thumbnail, process photo + reflection) | Humanises you, builds trust | Build-in-public, transition stories |
| **Tool teardown** ("I tested X, here's the verdict") | Clients use it for hiring research | After using a tool seriously for 1+ week |

## Format specs

- **Media**: images + video supported; recommended 1600 x 1200, max 200MB per asset
- **Editor blocks**: type `/` for blocks (quote, numbered list, animation, audio, code)
- **Character limit**: not strictly enforced; treat 800-1500 chars as the practical sweet spot
- **Skill tags**: tag every post with 3-5 skills it demonstrates (e.g. `nextjs`, `ai`, `saas`)
- **Hashtags**: skill tags ARE the hashtag system; no separate freeform hashtags

## Posting cadence

- **Floor**: 2 posts/week (Creator badge eligibility)
- **Growth phase**: 1/day for 14 days, then taper to 3-4/week
- **Steady state**: 3-4/week
- **Reply game**: comment on 3-5 posts/day in your skill tags; replies on Contra are weighted heavily because the active poster pool is small

## Hook formulas that work on Contra

- "Built [thing] in [timeframe]. Here's the stack and the screenshots."
- "[Tool] vs [tool] for [niche use case]: I shipped with both. Verdict."
- "Client asked for X. I delivered Y instead. Here's why it converted better."
- "5 things I wish I knew before going independent in [niche]."
- "Shipped [feature] this week. Process in 6 slides."
- "Took a [type] project from $0 to [result] in [timeframe]." (only if true)

## Anti-patterns (Contra-specific)

- "Available for hire" posts as primary content : readers tune out fast
- Cross-posted IG vertical 9:16 without resizing
- Walls of text with no media
- Inconsistent visual style across posts : your feed is your portfolio extension
- Asking for likes/shares : platform tone is professional
- Posting only when launching

## Atom-to-format map

| Atom present | Best Contra format |
|---|---|
| Hero screenshot + shipped feature | Build log carousel |
| Live URL of a working app | Single hero image + caption + link |
| 30-90s screen recording | Demo video post |
| Numbered process or lessons | Tutorial-style numbered post |
| Before/after data | Single image + caption with numbers |
| Personal transition / reflection | Journaling post with desk/process photo |
| Pure text take with no visual | Skip Contra; route to X instead |

## Repurposing INTO Contra

- **IG Reel** : strip vertical, repost as 60s desktop demo OR turn key frames into a 5-slide carousel
- **X thread** : reformat as numbered post with one hero image; expand each tweet to 1-2 sentences
- **Reddit case study** : near-direct lift; keep TL;DR, drop subreddit-specific jargon, add hero screenshot
- **Blog post** : carousel of the 5-7 main claims, each slide one claim + visual
- **YouTube tutorial** : embed Loom-style 60s clip or pull 3 timestamped GIFs into a carousel

## Repurposing FROM Contra

- Build log carousel : IG carousel (1:1 crop) + X thread (1 tweet per slide) + Reddit r/SideProject post
- Demo video : IG Reel (vertical reframe) + X video tweet + YouTube Short
- Tool teardown : Reddit comment in the tool's subreddit (different framing) + LinkedIn post (when in scope)

## Profile-as-landing-page rule

Contra is unique : every post links back to your profile, services, and work tab in one click. So every post should pass the "would this make a hiring client click my services tab?" check. If yes, post. If no, route the angle to a different platform.

## Creator program quick facts

- **Cadence floor**: 2 posts/week
- **Selection**: top 10% of posters reviewed weekly by Contra team
- **Reward**: Creator badge, amplified feed exposure, referral revenue from Pro signups via your posts
- **Distribution**: Contra may republish creator content to their own social channels

## Content pillar weight for Contra (vs other platforms)

| Pillar | Contra weight | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Mini-projects | 40% | Clients buy proof-of-shipping; build logs convert |
| Knowledge sharing | 35% | Demonstrates expertise without selling |
| Journaling | 20% | Build-in-public and transition stories work well |
| Memes/relatable | 5% | Use sparingly; only freelance-pain memes that double as commentary |
