# Funnels : TOFU / MOFU / BOFU

Not marketing jargon. A check to make sure every piece has a next step and the mix over time actually moves someone from stranger to buyer.

## Stage definitions (solo-creator edition)

| Stage | Goal | Audience | Content types |
|---|---|---|---|
| **TOFU (Top)** | Reach new eyes | Cold, curious strangers | Memes, viral hooks, broad tutorials, trend takes, SEO posts |
| **MOFU (Middle)** | Trust + prove you solve their problem | Warm, seen you before | Case studies, how-tos, comparisons, build-in-public, deep dives |
| **BOFU (Bottom)** | Move them to decide | Hot, ready to buy / hire / sign up | Testimonials, pricing breakdowns, objection handling, demos, urgency |

## Healthy content mix

- **60% TOFU** : always feed the top
- **30% MOFU** : nurture consideration
- **10% BOFU** : convert the ready

Only-BOFU = preaching to the convinced. Only-TOFU = entertainment with no revenue.

## Gaurav's pillars mapped to funnel

(Pillars read live from Pathrix via `list_workspace`. This mapping is the default starting point.)

| Pillar | Primary stage | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Memes** | TOFU | Viral dev/freelance sarcasm. Share > reach > network growth. |
| **Journaling** | MOFU | Build-in-public, project updates, lessons. Trust and authority. |
| **Mini Projects** | MOFU -> BOFU | Demos of capability. Trust + "hire this person" signal. |
| **Knowledge Sharing** | MOFU -> BOFU | Elite tutorials, frameworks. Positions as the expert. Converts to leads. |

Notes:
- Memes and Knowledge Sharing are the two extremes. Journaling and Mini Projects bridge the middle.
- For a balanced week: 4x TOFU-flavored posts, 3x MOFU, 1x BOFU. Adjust by traction.

## Content bridges (no dead-ends rule)

Every TOFU post must have a path to MOFU. Every MOFU must have a path to BOFU.

| From TOFU | Bridge to MOFU | Bridge to BOFU |
|---|---|---|
| Viral meme on pain | Tutorial series (email/DM) | Offer: "Here's the full system" |
| Trending topic take | In-depth breakdown post | "Want the consulting call?" |
| Short SEO tutorial | Lead magnet (checklist, template) | Case study: "How I got result X" |

## How to spot the stage of a draft

Ask:
1. Who is this for? (Stranger / someone who follows / someone about to pay?)
2. What's the intended action? (Scroll / save / DM / click?)
3. What's the next piece that builds on this?

If you can't answer all three, the draft is unfinished.

## Weekly funnel audit (5-minute check)

Pull last 7 days of posts. For each, label TOFU / MOFU / BOFU. If the ratio strays more than 15% from 60/30/10, rebalance next week.

## Anti-patterns

- Every post is MOFU "how-to" : reach flatlines, no new audience comes in.
- Every post is TOFU meme : followers grow, revenue doesn't.
- BOFU content with no audience warming : closes zero because nobody trusts you yet.
- Same CTA on every post : audience stops reading CTAs.
